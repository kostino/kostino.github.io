About me

I will be starting my PhD @ Stony Brook University in August ‘24. Currently, I’m doing research at the IDT Lab of ITI-CERTH, working with Dr Vasileios Mezaris. Previously, I graduated with an Integrated Master’s degree from the Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECE) department of the Aristotle University of Thessaloniki (AUTH), where I worked with Prof. Nestor Chatzidiamantis and Prof. George K. Karagiannidis.
